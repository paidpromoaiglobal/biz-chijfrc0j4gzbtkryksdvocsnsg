About Us

SHREE JI YOGA & FITNESS - YOGA, YOGA THERAPHY, DANCE, ZUMBA, MEDITATION, WEIGHT GAIN & LOSS

Shree Ji Yoga & Fitness is a premier wellness center located in the heart of Jhotwara, Jaipur, dedicated to helping individuals of all ages and fitness levels embark on a transformative health journey. Under the expert guidance of our experienced instructors, including the highly praised Suman ma'am, we offer a serene and energizing environment where every session is thoughtfully structured to nurture both the body and mind. With a perfect 5-star rating from our valued members, we take pride in delivering truly life-changing experiences.

From traditional yoga and yoga therapy to high-energy Zumba, classical dance, deep meditation, and customized weight gain and loss programs, our diverse offerings ensure there is something for everyone. Our studio is equipped with quality fitness equipment and radiates warm, positive energy that keeps our members coming back day after day. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, Shree Ji Yoga & Fitness welcomes you to begin or continue your wellness journey with us — open six days a week from 5:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
